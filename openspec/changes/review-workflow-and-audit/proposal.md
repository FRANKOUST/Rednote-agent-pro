## Why

The platform defaults to manual review, so editorial approval and auditability must exist before publish automation is trusted.

## What Changes

- Add approve/reject review workflow.
- Add review notes and publish-ready transitions.
- Add audit logging for key operator and system events.

## Capabilities

### New Capabilities

- `review-workflow`: Draft approval and rejection flow.
- `audit-trail`: Persistent audit log of key actions.

### Modified Capabilities

None.

## Impact

- Adds human-in-the-loop control and traceability.
